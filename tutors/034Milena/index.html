ABOUT ME
Hello! I'm Mila, and I love helping children discover languages through fun, creative online lessons.
I teach English, Spanish, and Bulgarian to young learners, and prepare students for Cambridge English exams (Pre-A1 Starters through B1 Preliminary). Speaking three languages and having lived in Bulgaria, Spain, and the UK, I truly understand the language learning journey.
I bring creativity, patience, and genuine care to every lesson. Let's make learning fun together!
TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
I believe children learn best when they're having fun and feel safe to make mistakes.
My online lessons are playful and interactive, using games, songs, stories, and visual materials. I follow the CLIL method, so we learn language through engaging topics like animals, nature, and everyday life rather than just grammar drills.
I adapt to each child's pace, celebrate small wins, and build confidence step by step.

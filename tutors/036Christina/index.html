ABOUT ME
I’m Christina, an American English teacher currently based in Gran Canaria, Spain. I’ve been teaching English to adults and teens for years, mainly working with international professionals who need English for real life, not textbooks.
I work with people who already know English but don’t always feel confident using it. My goal is simple: help you speak more clearly, more naturally, and with less stress. I’m friendly, direct, and practical, and I care a lot about making lessons feel useful and human.
TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
My teaching is highly personalized and conversation-driven. I don’t follow rigid lesson scripts or one-size-fits-all programs. Instead, I adapt each session to the student’s goals, level, and real communication needs.
I work mainly with adults and teens on speaking confidence, business English, interview skills, and IELTS preparation. Classes focus on clarity, structure, and natural expression — not perfection. I give clear feedback, explain why things work (or don’t), and help students build tools they can actually use outside the lesson.
If you want practical English, honest feedback, and a teacher who adjusts to you, that’s where I do my best work.

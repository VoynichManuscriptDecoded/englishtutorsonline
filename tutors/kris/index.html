ABOUT ME

If you’ve found your way here, you’re probably looking for some help with your English. I’m Kristine, an experienced English teacher with a unique background and an inspiring passion for teaching. I’m originally from Wales in the UK, but now reside in Portugal, where my husband and I manage an animal rescue project and sanctuary on a farm where we live “off the grid”.

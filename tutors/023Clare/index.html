ABOUT ME
Hello! I’m Clare and I’m from Cambridge, UK, but now I live and work in Seville in Spain.
I have 24 years of teaching experience, and I have taught students of all ages, from children to adults. Over the last few years, I have focused mainly on Business English, teaching both one-to-one classes and group classes in multinational companies.
I believe that every student is different, so I tailor my classes to each student’s needs, goals, and level. Whether you need English for work, exams, travel, or daily communication, I design lessons that are practical, clear, and motivating.
I have taught students from many different countries and cultures, both in person and online, and I have experience working on several online teaching platforms.
I also have strong experience in exam preparation, helping students successfully prepare for the Cambridge English exams and Trinity exams, from B1 to C2 level.
My aim is to help you feel confident, comfortable, and successful when using English in real life.

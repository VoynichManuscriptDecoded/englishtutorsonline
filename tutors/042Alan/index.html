TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
I have a friendly communicative approach to teaching. My aim is to make the student feel comfortable and confident to use the language that they already have (or have just learnt) and to build up vocabulary and grammar gradually in small natural steps. I encourage students to read engaging content or listen to podcasts or watch videos which leads to fun, interesting discussion, fluency and better language production in real life situations. My students are consistently happy with my teaching.

ABOUT ME
Hi, I’m Christine, a CELTA-qualified tutor from England with over 15 years of experience. I started teaching English in language schools 16 years ago while living in Bulgaria, before progressing to online teaching and I’ve never looked back. Now living back in the UK, I enjoy the flexibility of teaching online and the opportunity to meet students from all over the world. I’ve truly met some fantastic people and love celebrating their successes with them. TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
My teaching approach is student-centred, supportive, and practical. I believe every learner is different, so my lessons are carefully tailored to each student’s goals, level, and learning style. In my 1-to-1 online lessons, I focus on building confidence as well as accuracy, with plenty of speaking practice and clear, constructive feedback. I support students with general English, business communication, and exam preparation (including IELTS and Cambridge), always adapting lessons to suit their individual needs. I aim to create a relaxed and encouraging learning environment where students feel comfortable making mistakes and asking questions, helping them make steady, real progress.

TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
My aim in teaching is to assist students to achieve their goals and improve their skill levels. At the same time I would also want to build a relationship of trust with students so that they feel free to interact and communicate in an open way. I also know that one method of teaching does not have the same effect on all the students and therefore I make use of different methods. I want students to regard the learning process as a journey of discovery. Sometimes the road is easy and familiar and sometimes it is more complicated. My function is to guide students along this road. It is my responsibility to determine what their goals are and to plan and design my sessions in such a way that they will achieve these goals.

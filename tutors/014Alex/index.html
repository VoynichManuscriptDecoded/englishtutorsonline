TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
My approach is learner-centred and flexible, shaped around each student’s goals, interests and learning style. I take time to diagnose areas of difficulty—such as grammar, pronunciation or fluency—and use this to create personalised study plans.
I aim to make my lessons as engaging and fun as possible, with a strong focus on real-world English, and I regularly use materials such as articles, videos, and current topics to keep learning relevant and meaningful, helping students use English naturally and confidently outside the classroom.

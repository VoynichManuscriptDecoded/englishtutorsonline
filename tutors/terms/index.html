Terms & Conditions

Scope of Services

  • English Tutors Online operates as an independent teachers’ marketplace.
  • The platform lists independent English teachers who provide online English language instruction directly to students.
  • All lessons are delivered online via video conferencing.
  • Teachers operate as independent service providers, not employees of English Tutors Online.
  • Lesson content, teaching methods, availability, and delivery are the responsibility of the individual teacher.
  • All teachers are known to at least one other teacher on the platform, are recommended, and are proven professionals.
  • English Tutors Online operates a curated marketplace capped at a maximum of 100 teachers to ensure quality, clarity, and a manageable choice for students.

English Tutors Online provides:

  • The marketplace platform
  • Teacher listings
  • A shared pricing and policy framework
  • A contact point for serious issues or concerns

English Tutors Online does not directly provide teaching services.

Payment & Fees

  • English Tutors Online sets a shared marketplace pricing structure.
  • Teachers may choose whether to offer single lessons, lesson packages, or informal arrangements agreed privately with students.
  • No teacher may charge more than the prices listed on the platform.
  • Payment is made directly between the teacher and the student using their agreed method.
  • Teaching arrangements such as lesson frequency, package size, or informal sessions are a private matter between the teacher and the student.
  • If a student is asked to pay more than the listed prices, they should contact English Tutors Online.

Lesson Scheduling

  • All lessons offered through English Tutors Online are 30 minutes in duration.
  • Lesson times and frequency are agreed directly between the teacher and the student.
  • Any changes, cancellations, or rescheduling must be communicated directly with the teacher.
  • English Tutors Online does not manage lesson bookings, cancellations, payments, or refunds.
  • Emails regarding late attendance, missed lessons, or routine scheduling matters will not be reviewed.
  • Attendance, missed lessons, and lesson forfeiture are private matters agreed between teachers and students and are not managed by English Tutors Online.
  • If a teacher repeatedly fails to attend lessons as agreed or does not provide lessons without reasonable explanation, this should be reported to English Tutors Online.
  • English Tutors Online is not financially liable for disputes, missed lessons, cancellations, or refunds arising from agreements made directly between teachers and students.

Code of Conduct

  • Teachers must act professionally and respectfully toward students.
  • Teachers must comply with marketplace pricing and conduct standards.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behaviour will not be tolerated.
  • English Tutors Online reserves the right to remove teachers from the platform who do not comply with these standards.

Termination

  • Teachers may request removal from the platform at any time.
  • English Tutors Online may remove a teacher if marketplace standards are not followed.
  • Termination does not affect private arrangements between teachers and students.
  • English Tutors Online is not responsible for refunds or unresolved matters following termination.

Governing Law

  • These terms are governed by the applicable laws of the teacher’s country of residence.

Terms & Conditions

Scope of Services

  • The services provided include online English language instruction via video conferencing.
  • Lessons are conducted by the teacher at agreed-upon times and durations.

Lesson Scheduling

  • Both the teacher and the student agree to follow the agreed schedule.
  • Any changes or cancellations must be communicated at least 6 hours before the scheduled lesson.

Lesson Content

  • Curriculum, materials, and lesson plans are provided by the teacher.
  • Students may request specific topics or materials in advance.

Confidentiality

  • Both parties agree not to disclose any confidential information shared during lessons.
  • This includes personal information, lesson content, or any sensitive data.

Recording Policy

  • Lessons may not be recorded without explicit consent from both teacher and student.
  • If recording is agreed upon, this must be confirmed before the lesson begins.

Payment and Fees

  • Payment for lessons must be made in advance via the chosen payment platform.
  • Any additional fees will be communicated beforehand.

Cancellation and Refunds

  • Cancellations made within 6 hours of the lesson are non-refundable.
  • Rescheduling is possible if made before the 6-hour notice period.
  • A full refund is available within 14 days of purchasing a session pack if no lessons have been used.
  • Refund requests can be made by contacting us directly.

Technical Requirements

  • Both parties are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection and appropriate equipment.

Responsibilities

  • The teacher will provide instruction to the best of their abilities.
  • The student agrees to participate actively, complete assignments, and practise outside lesson times.

Code of Conduct

  • Respectful behaviour and language are expected from both parties.
  • Discrimination, harassment, or inappropriate conduct will not be tolerated.

Termination

  • Either party may terminate lessons with 6 hours' notice.
  • Refunds for unused prepaid lessons will be provided on a prorated basis if applicable.

Governing Law

  • These terms and conditions are governed by applicable laws under the tutor’s country of residence.

Privacy Policy

Who we are

Our website address is: https://englishtutorsonline.org

Personal Data

Any personal information provided by students (such as name, email or WhatsApp) is used solely for lesson communication and scheduling.

Cookies

Third-party services used on this site (such as booking platforms or embedded tools) may use cookies in accordance with their own policies.

Embedded Content

Pages may include embedded content (e.g., booking widgets or videos). These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with external parties except where necessary to provide the tutoring services requested by students.

Data Retention

Any personal information provided directly to tutors is retained only for teaching, scheduling and communication purposes.

Your Rights

Students may request to update or delete personal information at any time, unless it must be retained for legal or administrative reasons.

Contact

For any privacy-related requests, please contact any of the tutors using the email addresses provided on the Tutors page.

Terms & Conditions

Scope of Services

  • English Tutors Online operates as an independent teachers’ marketplace.
  • All lessons are delivered online via video conferencing.
  • Teachers are independent service providers, not employees of English Tutors Online.
  • Lesson content, delivery, and availability are the responsibility of the individual teacher.

Payment & Fees

  • English Tutors Online provides a shared pricing framework for the marketplace.
  • Payment is made directly between the teacher and the student.
  • No teacher may charge more than the prices listed on the platform.
  • Any pricing concerns should be reported immediately.

Lesson Scheduling

  • All lessons are 30 minutes unless otherwise agreed privately.
  • Scheduling, cancellations, and rescheduling are handled directly with the teacher.
  • English Tutors Online does not manage bookings, payments, or refunds.

Code of Conduct

  • Professional and respectful behaviour is required at all times.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate conduct will not be tolerated.
  • Teachers must adhere to marketplace standards and pricing rules.

Termination

  • Teachers may be removed for breaches of conduct or pricing rules.
  • Termination ends platform listing access only.
  • Private teacher–student agreements remain unaffected.

Governing Law

  • Agreements are governed by the laws of the teacher’s country of residence.

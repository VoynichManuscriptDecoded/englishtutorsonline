ABOUT ME
Hi, I’m Nicole — an English tutor helping adults and teens speak with confidence in real-life and professional situations.
I’ve been teaching English for over three years, working with learners from A1 to C1. With a background in Psychology and HR, I focus on how people learn, communicate, and build confidence — especially at work, in interviews, presentations, and exams.
My lessons are practical, personalised, and confidence-focused. I help students understand their mistakes, speak more naturally, and use English clearly in real situations — not just in textbooks.
If you’re looking for supportive, structured lessons that help you actually use English, you’re in the right place.
TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
I believe students learn best when lessons feel relevant, supportive, and practical.
My approach focuses on real communication — not just grammar rules. Lessons are structured but flexible, with clear explanations, guided practice, and plenty of speaking time. I help students understand why mistakes happen, so they can correct themselves and feel more confident using English independently.
I adapt every lesson to the learner’s goals, level, and pace — whether that’s preparing for an exam, improving workplace communication, or speaking more naturally in everyday situations.
The aim is simple: clarity, confidence, and progress you can feel.

ABOUT ME
Hi, I'm Alison and I'm a native English speaker from the UK. I am a fully qualified and experienced language teacher with over 20 years of experience teaching English at all levels.
Lessons are all completely tailored to individual requirements and with the aim of encouraging you to feel much more confident from the very first lesson.
I have a BA degree in Politics from the University of Sussex and a Post Graduate Certificate of Education in Languages from the University of Sheffield.
I have taught English to all ages, groups and individuals, and I have been teaching languages online for about ten years now.
I have experience in General English (from complete Beginners to Advanced level), IELTS, Cambridge exam preparation, Business English and English for Academic Purposes.
A qualified teacher, I have also worked in various creative industries, including Film, Television and Public Relations. I understand the needs of a fast-based corporate sector and have trained business executives from all over the world to help them improve their communication and presentation skills and succeed in the workplace. I love learning languages myself and speak French, German and Spanish, and am currently learning Italian. I am passionate about languages and like to think that I pass that enthusiasm on to my students.

TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
I specialise in accent reduction mostly for C1+ students who feel stuck at their current level and really want to make the leap to full proficiency/near native level. I have proven methods,using specially designed material, involving drills, discussion and reading out loud, which I have used for several years with my students from all around the world to produce the desired result - to be able to speak the best and clearest English possible! During a class with me, as well as helping you with your accent, I will at all times carefully correct your grammar and vocabulary too, always suggesting more natural idioms, everything being carefully logged in a google doc, which you can also edit. eg add homework or make notes. If you decide to work with me, specifically on accent reduction, I supply you with clear to follow audios after each lesson. I believe all of the above give me the qualities needed to be a great teacher! A very clear to follow approach, a teacher with patience, kindness but a good dose of fun thrown in for good measure!

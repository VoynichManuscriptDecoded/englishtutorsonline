ABOUT ME
I’m an English teacher with over 13 years of experience, and I’m passionate about connecting with people from different cultures through language. I grew up learning two languages and have always been fascinated by how the brain learns and processes language. Before teaching, I was a certified fitness trainer and nutritionist and mostly created meal and exercise plans for clients. However, my love of learning and languages ultimately led me to education. Getting to know my students allows me to create personalised lessons while continuing to learn and grow alongside them
TEACHING APPROACH & WHAT TO EXPECT
My teaching style is patient, friendly, and student-centred. I adapt lessons to each learner’s background, goals, and first language, encouraging students to speak as much as possible while I guide and support them. Lessons are interactive and practical, using a variety of resources to keep students engaged. I focus not only on teaching English, but also on helping students develop the skills and habits needed to continue learning independently. I believe language learning is a long-term journey, and I support students with realistic expectations and consistent progress.

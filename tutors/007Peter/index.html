ABOUT ME
I have been teaching English online for about seven years. My students range from School students to Scientists. I studied History in college and for many years I worked repairing and maintaining aircraft. So my interests are varied! I have trained extensively in public speaking and have many years experience. I am flexible when it comes to teaching. Some students want a rigid, structured approach, while others want to have an open conversation and learn in a natural, organic way. I do my best to suit the students' style and teach in a way that is most effective and comfortable for them.
